## Applications

### Applications of SRR-99 include:

- Gas turbine engines: Used in turbine blades and vanes for advanced aero engines.
- High-temperature environments: Suitable for applications requiring strength and durability at elevated temperatures.
- Replacement for DS MM002: Designed as a high-strength alternative in applications where increased creep, tensile, and fatigue strength are required

---

## Properties

**Mechanical properties:**

- High creep strength, with creep lives at least ten times those of DS MM002 at 1000°C.
- At least 15% improvement in yield and low cycle fatigue strength compared to DS MM002.
- Good thermal fatigue resistance

**Microstructure:**

- Features γ' (gamma prime) precipitates as the main strengthening phase
- Designed to have a high anti-phase boundary energy, promoting stacking fault mode of creep deformation up to about 850°C
- Fabricability: Can be cast as single crystals using directional solidification techniques4.
- Heat treatment: Designed to have a wide heat treatment window9.
- Metallurgical stability: Formulated for good stability at high temperatures9.
- Density: Approximately 8.56 g/cm³5.
- Liquidus temperature: 1356°C4.
