---
title: Rene 80H
description: René 80H is a variant of René 80, a polycrystalline nickel-based superalloy, optimized for high-temperature applications in gas turbines. The "H" designation typically refers to modifications such as higher hafnium content or other adjustments to improve specific properties like creep resistance, oxidation stability, or grain boundary strengthening. René 80H is a polycrystalline nickel-based superalloy designed for turbine blades, vanes, and other components operating in high-temperature environments. It is strengthened by γ' (gamma prime) precipitates and grain boundary carbides, with hafnium (Hf) additions to enhance grain boundary properties.
