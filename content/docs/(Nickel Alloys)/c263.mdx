## Properties

- Temperature capability: Maintains high strength up to 816°C (1500°F) and good oxidation resistance up to 982°C (1800°F)
- Solution treatment: 1150 ± 10°C (2100 ± 15°F) for 5-15 minutes (sheet and plate) or 0.5-2.5 hours (rod and bar), followed by water quenching or air cooling
- Precipitation heat treatment: 800 ± 15°C (1475 ± 25°F) for 8 hours ± 0.5 h, air cooled

**Mechanical:**

- High strength at elevated temperatures
- Good ductility and formability in the annealed condition

**Microstructure:**

- Features γ (gamma) matrix strengthened by γ' (gamma prime) precipitates
- Contains MC carbides rich in Ti and Mo, and M23C6 carbides rich in Cr, Ni, Mo, and Co

**Physical properties:**

- Density: 8.36 g/cm³ (0.302 lbs/in³)14
- Melting range: 1300-1355°C4
- Non-magnetic
