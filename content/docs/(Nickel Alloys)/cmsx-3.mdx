## Properties

**Casting and processing:**

- Excellent castability, allowing for the production of complex shapes and large castings.
- Can be heat treated to optimize the size and distribution of γ' precipitates.
- The low carbon content improves weldability compared to higher carbon variants.
- Designed for single crystal casting.
- Responds well to heat treatment for optimizing properties

  **Microstructure:**

- Features γ (gamma) matrix strengthened by γ' (gamma prime) precipitates.
- Contains approximately 60% volume fraction of γ' phase.

  **Physical properties:**

- Density: 8.62 g/cm³ (0.311 lbs/cu. in).
- Exhibits good creep resistance at high temperatures.
- Shows lower creep resistance compared to CMSX-4 due to the absence of rhenium.
- Utilizes both solid solution strengthening and precipitation hardening.
- The absence of rhenium results in lower creep resistance compared to later generations of superalloys like CMSX-4
