## Applications

#### Waspalloy finds applications in various industries, including:

- Aerospace: Widely used in gas turbine engine components such as blades, disks, shafts, and seals.
- Power generation: Applied in industrial gas turbines.
- Automotive: Used in high-temperature engine components.
- Missile systems: Employed in various components.
- Fasteners: Used in high-temperature applications

---

## Properties

- Temperature capability: Maintains strength and oxidation resistance up to 980°C (1800°F).
- Mechanical properties: Offers high tensile strength (up to 200 ksi or 1380 MPa) and excellent creep resistance.
- Corrosion resistance: Provides good resistance to oxidation and corrosion in combustion environments.
- Fabricability: Can be forged, cast, and heat-treated to achieve desired properties
