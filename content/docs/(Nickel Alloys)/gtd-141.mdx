## Applications

### Applications of GTD-141 include:

- Gas turbine engine components, particularly:
  - First-stage turbine blades.
  - High-pressure turbine blades.
- Industrial gas turbines.
- Advanced aerospace propulsion systems.

---

## Properties

- Superior high-temperature strength compared to equiaxed cast alloys.
- Excellent creep resistance, particularly in the longitudinal direction.
- Enhanced oxidation and hot corrosion resistance.
- Improved thermal fatigue resistance due to DS structure.
- Optimized for long-term stability in gas turbine environments.

**Microstructure**:

- Columnar grain structure due to directional solidification.
- γ (gamma) matrix with coherent γ' (gamma prime) precipitates.
- Carefully controlled carbide distribution.
- Minimal formation of detrimental phases (e.g., TCP phases).

**Mechanical Properties**

- High-temperature strength superior to conventional cast alloys.
- Excellent creep resistance.
- Good low cycle fatigue (LCF) and thermal mechanical fatigue (TMF) properties.
- Specific values not publicly available due to proprietary nature.
