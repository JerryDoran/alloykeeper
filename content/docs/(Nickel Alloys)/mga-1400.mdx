title: MGA-1400
description: MGA 1400 is a nickel-based superalloy developed for high-temperature applications in gas turbine engines. MGA 1400's combination of high-temperature strength, creep resistance, and oxidation resistance makes it an excellent choice for advanced gas turbine applications, particularly in high-stress, high-temperature environments such as turbine blades and vanes. The alloy is optimized for high-temperature strength, creep resistance, and oxidation resistance, maintaining structural integrity at temperatures up to about 1400°F (760°C) or higher.
