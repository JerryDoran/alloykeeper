## Applications

#### Alloy 617 finds applications in various industries, including:

- Combustion cans, and transition liners in both aircraft and land-based gas turbines.
- It's also used in the petrochemical industry and for components of power-generating plants, both fossil-fueled and nuclear.

---

## Properties

<ul>
  <li>Excellent mechanical and creep properties up to 1,100°C (2,000°F).</li>
  <li>Resistance to a wide range of corrosive environments.</li>
  <li>Good fabricability and weldability using conventional techniques.</li>
  <li>Solid-solution strengthening imparted by cobalt and molybdenum.</li>
</ul>
