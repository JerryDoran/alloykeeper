## Applications

### Applications of ECY-768 include:

- Gas Turbines: Nozzle guide vanes, combustor liners.
- Medical Implants: Hip/knee replacements (if biocompatible).
- Industrial: Wear plates, valve seats, pump components.
- Aerospace: Exhaust systems, afterburner components.
