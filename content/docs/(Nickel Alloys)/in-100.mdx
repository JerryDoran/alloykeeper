## Applications

### Applications of Inco-100 include:

IN-100 alloy is primarily used in high-temperature applications in gas turbine engines, particularly for components in the hot sections. The main applications include:

- Turbine blades
- Turbine vanes
- Turbine nozzles
- Integral wheels

---

## Properties

- Maintains high rupture strength through 1900°F (1038°C).
- Density: 0.280 lb/cu. in. (7.75 g/cu. cm).
- Melting range: 2305-2435°F (1260-1335°C).
- Precipitation hardenable, vacuum cast alloy
- Patented (Patent #3,061,426) and produced under license from The International Nickel Company, Inc.

**Mechanical properties:**

- High rupture strength at elevated temperatures.
- Attractive strength-to-density ratio due to high Al and Ti content and low refractory metal content.

  **Microstructure:**

- Features γ (gamma) matrix strengthened by γ' (gamma prime) precipitates.
- High percentages of aluminum and titanium contribute to strengthening.
