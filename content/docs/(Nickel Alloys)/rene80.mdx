## Composition

- Ni: 60.0-61%
- Cr: 14%
- Co: 9.5%
- Ti: 4-5%
- Mo: 4%
- W: 4%
- Al: 3%
- C: 0.15-0.17%
- B: 0.015%
- Zr: 0.03%

---

## Applications

#### Rene 80 finds applications in various industries, including:

- Aerospace: Used in gas turbine hot section components, particularly turbine blades.
- Power generation: Applied in industrial gas turbines.
- High-temperature environments: Suitable for applications requiring strength and corrosion resistance at elevated temperatures

---

## Properties

- Temperature capability: Maintains strength and oxidation resistance up to 950°C (1742°F) for long-term service.
- Mechanical properties: Offers high tensile strength (1158 MPa), good yield strength (1034 MPa), and excellent elongation (15%).
- Corrosion resistance: Provides excellent oxidation resistance, with only 0.41 mg/cm2 of specific mass gain after 100 hours of oxidation.
- Fabricability: Can be cast and heat-treated to achieve desired properties
