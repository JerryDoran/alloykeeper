## Applications

### Applications of RENE N515 include:

- Aerospace Gas Turbines:

  - Turbine vanes and combustor liners.
  - High-pressure turbine (HPT) blades in jet engines (e.g., GE90, GEnx).

- Rotor blades in land-based power generators.
