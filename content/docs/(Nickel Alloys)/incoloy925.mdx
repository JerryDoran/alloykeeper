## Applications

#### Incoloy 925 finds applications in various industries, including:

- Oil and gas: Used in drilling operations, offshore production, and transportation of oil and gas
- Power generation: Applied in turbines and boilers.
- Aerospace: Utilized in aircraft engines and high-temperature components.
- Chemical and petrochemical: Employed in corrosive material transport and processing equipment.
- Nuclear industry: Used in nuclear reactors due to its high temperature and radiation resistance.

---

## Properties

- Outstanding corrosion resistance in both oxidizing and reducing environments
- High strength, especially after age-hardening heat treatment
- Excellent resistance to stress corrosion cracking, pitting, and crevice corrosion
- Good fabricability and weldability
- Ability to maintain properties at elevated temperatures
