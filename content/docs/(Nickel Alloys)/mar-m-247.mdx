## Applications

### Applications of MAR-M-247 include:

- Gas turbine engine components (e.g., turbine blades, vanes).
- Aerospace systems.
- Aircraft components.
- Turbocharger wheels.
- High-pressure turbine parts.
- Combustor/exhaust components.

## Properties

- High strength at elevated temperatures.
- Excellent creep resistance.
- Good oxidation resistance.
- Balanced composition for optimal mechanical properties.
- Investment casting for complex geometries.
- Vacuum induction melting (VIM) followed by investment casting.
- Heat Treatment: Solution treatment and aging to optimize γ' precipitation.
- Challenging to process via welding or Selective Laser Melting (SLM) due to crack formation.
- High-temperature Selective Laser Melting (HT-SLM) with preheating up to 1200°C has been explored to reduce cracking.

  **Microstructure**:

- Consists of a γ (gamma) matrix phase and γ' (gamma prime) precipitates.
- MC carbides (primarily Ti, Ta, Hf, and W).
- Possible M23C6 carbides in grain boundaries after heat treatment.
- "Primary γ' islands" with eutectic structure in interdendritic regions.
