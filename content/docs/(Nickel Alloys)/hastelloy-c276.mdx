---
title: Hastelloy C-276
description: Hastelloy C-276 is a versatile nickel-chromium-molybdenum superalloy designed for exceptional corrosion resistance in harsh environments. Its composition includes approximately 57% nickel, 15-17% molybdenum, 14.5-16.5% chromium, and smaller amounts of tungsten, iron, and other elements.
---

---

## Composition

- Ni: 57%
- Cr: 14.5-16.5%
- Mo: 15-17%
- Fe: 4-7%
- W: 3-4.5%
- Co: 2.5% max
- Mn: 1% max
- C: 0.01% max
- V: 0.35% max
- Si: 0.08% max
- P: 0.04% max
- S: 0.03% max

---

## Applications

#### Hastelloy C-276 finds applications in various industries, including:

- Chemical processing: Used in reactors, heat exchangers, and piping systems
- Oil and gas: Employed in offshore platforms and refineries
- Power generation: Applied in boilers and condensers
- Pharmaceutical: Utilized in reactors and storage tanks
- Waste treatment and pollution control: Used in flue gas desulfurization systems
- Pulp and paper production: Applied in digesters and bleach areas

---

## Properties

- Outstanding resistance to both oxidizing and reducing media
- Excellent resistance to pitting, crevice corrosion, and stress corrosion cracking
- High strength and ductility
- Good fabricability and weldability
- Low carbon content, minimizing carbide precipitation during welding
