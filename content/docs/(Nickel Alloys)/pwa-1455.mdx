Its composition is optimized for cast applications, particularly in turbine blades where high-temperature strength and creep resistance are crucial

## Applications

### Applications of PWA-1455 include:

- Used in gas turbine engine components, particularly in hot sections.
- Suitable for cast turbine blades.

---

## Properties

- Can be investment cast.
- Research has been conducted on modifying casting parameters to improve high-temperature ductility.
- Maintains properties up to 1302°C (2375°F).

**Mechanical properties:**

  - Designed for high-temperature strength and creep resistance.

  **Microstructure:**

  - Features γ (gamma) matrix strengthened by γ' (gamma prime) precipitates, typical of nickel-based superalloys.
  - Likely contains carbides for additional strengthening.
