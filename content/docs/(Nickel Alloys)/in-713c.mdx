---
title: IN-713C
description: IN 713C is a nickel-based cast superalloy widely used in high-temperature applications, particularly for gas turbine components. It offers a balance of strength, oxidation resistance, and castability, making it suitable for static and rotating parts in aerospace and industrial engines.  IN 713C is a polycrystalline, precipitation-hardened alloy designed for investment casting. It is strengthened by γ' (gamma prime) precipitates and optimized for moderate-temperature service (up to ~870°C).

## Applications

### Applications of INCO-713C include:

- Aerospace: Turbine blades, vanes, and nozzle guide vanes.
- Turbine wheels and housings.
- Rotor and stator parts in power-generation systems.
- Turbocharger components: Suitable for high-temperature, corrosive environments.
